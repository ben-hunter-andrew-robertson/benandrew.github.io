NZ Sika App Privacy Policy

This app collects user submitted data for the purpose of gathering statistics on Sika deer in New Zealand.

The app requests the following permissions:

- Location (to simplify the data submission process)
- Camera (allows photos to be uploaded as evidence to the statistics provided by users)
- Gallery (allows photos to be uploaded as evidence to the statistics provided by users)

Personal and gathered data is only shared within the Fiordland Wapiti Foundation and the application developer.
